    5. Payment and Refund Policy

  • Sakayna aims to provide a seamless and secure payment experience for all users. This policy outlines the guidelines for processing payments, issuing refunds, and fare adjustments.
  • a. Payment Processing
    • Payment Method: Upon successful completion of a booking, the commuter is required to pay the driver either in cash or via GCash to GCash transfer.
    • Secure Transactions: For GCash transactions, all payments are processed securely through the GCash platform, which adheres to industry-standard security protocols.
    • Driver's Coin Deduction: When the driver accepts a booking, a specified amount of Sakay coins will be deducted from their account as a booking confirmation fee.
    • Coin Refund on Cancellation: If the commuter cancels the booking after the driver has accepted it, the deducted Sakay coins will be refunded back to the driver’s account.
